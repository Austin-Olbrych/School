Welcome to the Ski and Board club homepage

Fun and exciting club to ski or snowboard with friends

Everything you will need to know about the club can be found throughout this webpage. Hopefully it will allow you to gain the knowledge you will need if you are interested in joing, need to contact someone, or see possible trips that will be planned.


Here is a list of possible places we will go and visit

  • Alpine Valley
  • Mount Pleasant of Edinboro
  • Boston Mills & Brandywine
  • Snow Trails
  • Seven Springs Mountain

This organization is very fun for students to be apart of. We can help students gain the skills from friends instead of paying for actual lessions. It is cheaper this way, which is very nice because almost all of the expencies for this club is out of pocket. This organization is to help coordinate trips but also find deals for students if they do participate.
