Trips

Where?

The trips we plan for our organization are usually planned to be close by because students are busy with many things and aren't interested in driving very far during the week. If you look at the links on the right you can see some of the possible places we will go.


When?

We mostly plan trips on weekends when students have more free-time, but also will go during the week because we can find cheaper prices for lift tickets. Saving money is one aspect we really focus on because we understand that students aren't interested in paying a lot go snowboarding, especially if they are beginners.
