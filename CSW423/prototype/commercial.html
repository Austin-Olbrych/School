Communication and Experience will help successfully lead your project to completion.

We are equipped to meet the high expectations of your commercial electrical needs. We can handle retail, financial and food service spaces, educational facilities, office buildings, new or complete remodels, institutional projects and all similar needs. First Choice will begin each project with start-up meetings to implement the customers vision, and allow our field representative to be fully informed before work is commenced. By implementing strong communication channels from the start we are able to reduce time delays and change orders for our customers, while maintaining current information transfer to the project site.

Examples of Commercial upgrades, installations, and services.

  • Interior and Exterior Lighting Design and Installation
  • Data center and computer room power systems
  • Parking Lot /Street Lighting - Design and Installation
  • Lighting Upgrades
  • Pole Base Installations
  • Uninterruptible power supplies (UPS)
  • Electric heat trace installation and service
  • Generators - Design and Installation Services
  • Trenching and Underground Electrical Installations
  • Power conditioning transformer installation/service
  • HVAC and freezer equipment connection
