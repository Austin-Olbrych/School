Ever since the first time I went snowboarding I fell in love with it. Once I entered high school I immediately signed up to join our schools ski club. My freshman year was fun, but I was a little rusty so it took me some practice to actually stay up. I wasn't able to participate my sophomore or junior year because I had back problems but I did join again my senior year. However, I did hurt myself during one of our trips to Peak'n Peek. My friend is much better then me but somehow he talked me into going off a couple of jumps. So I tried it and the outcome wasn't very good.
