Solution

There aren’t many solutions to the Ebola disease besides doctors and scientist coming up with a cure. However, first I would recommend that you learn the signs for Ebola and see a doctor if you start showing signs or if someone you know starts showing signs. Also, don’t be too careful, if you have the symptoms don’t just think it is nothing, you would rather be safe than sorry. When it comes to disease outbreaks such as this you must stay informed of the current state of that disease. Next, watching the news and reading articles online will help you stay informed of what the new possible risk could be. Last, always perform basic hygiene by washing your hands, taking showers, washing off food and such before eating, and use disinfecting wipes to clean off surfaces to prevent the spread of that disease.
